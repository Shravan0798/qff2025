Explore quantum computing at IISc x Qiskit Fall Fest 2025!

Talks, Hands-on sessions, and Hackathon for curious minds.

About the Event

The six-day program will include lectures and hands-on sessions ranging from basics to Quantum Machine Learning, Quantum Optimization, Fault Tolerance, and Quantum Error Correction, led by researchers from IBM Quantum and IISc. All sessions will be held in hybrid mode.

The event will culminate in a 24-hour Hackathon based on Qiskit with prompts spanning various applications of quantum computing. More details about the hackathon will be announced soon — Hackathon Details

Other than the hackthon, daily challenges will be provided to keep participants engaged, earning them a spot on a leaderboard based on their performance. Top performers will win IBM Quantum swag.

Participants from IISc can attended the sessions in-person and participants from outside will be able to join all sessions online. All participants will receive participation certificates from IBM Quantum.
